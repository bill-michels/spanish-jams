About Mind Left Body Game

Mind Left Body Game is a fun app built for Deadheads to test their show and song knowledge. See if you can identify the year of a show just by listening and how your skills compare to others.

You need to create an account if you want to save your score and compete on the global leaderboard.

How It Works

Each round, the game pulls a random live song and your job is to guess the year.

You get three tries per round:

  • First guess correct: 3 points
  • Second guess correct: 2 points
  • Third guess correct: 1 point

Your all time score is tracked, and we will also give you stats on your current round.

We did our best to limit "tuning" and random other tracks that are not really capturing the show.

There are also some fun easter eggs.

The Music

All audio in Mind Left Body Game comes from the Grateful Dead collection on the Internet Archive, one of the largest freely available archives of live concert recordings in the world.
