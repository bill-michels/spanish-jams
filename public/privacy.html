Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 31, 2026

Mind Left Body Game ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website mindleftbodygame.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our site and play our game.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect a username and password. Passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plain text.
  • Game Data: We record your game scores and gameplay statistics to maintain our leaderboard.
  • Usage Data: We may collect standard web server logs including IP addresses, browser type, referring pages, and timestamps.
  • Cookies: We use session cookies to keep you logged in during your visit. These cookies are essential for the site to function and expire after 24 hours.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Mind Left Body Game experience
  • To manage your account and display your scores on the leaderboard
  • To improve our game and website
  • To detect and prevent abuse or unauthorized access

Third-Party Services

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our site. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting aboutads.info.

Internet Archive

Our game streams audio from the Internet Archive's Grateful Dead collection. When you play the game, your browser connects directly to archive.org to stream audio content. Please review the Internet Archive's Terms of Service for information about their data practices.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your personal information. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt encryption. Sessions are secured with HTTP-only cookies. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your account information and game scores for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact page.
